Pitra Dosh

In Vedic astrology, certain planetary combinations in a birth chart and lines on the palm indicate the presence of ancestral curses, known as Pitru Dosha or Matru Dosha. This affliction can bring sorrow, childlessness, untimely death, career difficulties, and various illnesses to the entire family. These defects can be remedied through specific rituals such as Tripindi Shraddha and Narayan Bali Shraddha, performed by Vedic scholars in Kashi (Varanasi).
